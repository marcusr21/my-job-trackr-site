What is Job Tracking?

Job tracking is the process of recording and monitoring every job application you submit throughout your job search. It involves logging company names, job titles, application dates, interview schedules, and offer details in one centralised location so nothing falls through the cracks.

My Job Trackr is a job tracking app purpose-built for this. It replaces scattered spreadsheets and browser bookmarks with a single dashboard where job seekers can track applications, manage interviews, set deadline reminders, and compare offers side-by-side. The app also includes native job search, so you can find and track opportunities without switching between platforms.

Unlike spreadsheets, a dedicated job tracking tool like My Job Trackr provides automated reminders, interview scheduling, analytics on your application progress, and mobile access from any device. Job seekers who track their applications systematically are better organised, follow up on time, and make more informed decisions when offers arrive.
