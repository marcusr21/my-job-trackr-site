What is a Job Tracker?

A job tracker is a tool designed to record and monitor every job application you submit throughout your job search. Instead of using scattered spreadsheets or notes, a job tracker app logs company names, job titles, application dates, interview schedules, and offer details in one centralised location so nothing falls through the cracks. Job tracking is essential for anyone applying to multiple positions.

My Job Trackr is a job tracker app purpose-built for serious job seekers. It replaces browser bookmarks and messy spreadsheets with a single dashboard where you can track job applications, manage interviews, set deadline reminders, and compare offers side-by-side. The app also includes native job search, so you can find and track opportunities without switching between platforms. Whether you call it a jobs tracker, job tracking app, or career tracking tool, My Job Trackr has you covered.

Unlike spreadsheets, a dedicated job tracker like My Job Trackr provides automated reminders, interview scheduling, analytics on your application progress, and mobile access from any device.
