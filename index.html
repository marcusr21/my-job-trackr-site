Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about My Job Trackr

What is My Job Trackr?

My Job Trackr is a job tracking application designed to help job seekers organise their job search. The app allows users to track job applications, manage interviews, search for jobs within the platform, and compare multiple job offers side-by-side. My Job Trackr provides a centralised platform where job seekers can keep all their application details, deadlines, and interview schedules in one place.

How much does My Job Trackr cost?

My Job Trackr costs ¬£2.99 per month for web users and ¬£3.99 per month through the iOS App Store. The pricing includes unlimited job tracking, native job searching, interview management, and offer comparison features.

What features does My Job Trackr include?

My Job Trackr includes native job searching to find opportunities within the app, unlimited job application tracking with status updates, interview calendar management with deadline reminders, job offer comparison tools to evaluate multiple offers side-by-side, Quick Import to auto-fill job details from URLs, detailed note-taking for each application, and application analytics to track your job search progress.

Is My Job Trackr available on iOS and Android?

My Job Trackr is currently available on iOS through the App Store and as a web application accessible from any browser. Android support is planned for future release. iOS users can download the native app from the App Store, whilst web users can access all features through their browser at app.myjobtrackr.com.

How does My Job Trackr help with job searching?

My Job Trackr helps job seekers by providing native job search functionality that allows users to find opportunities directly within the app. Users can search for jobs, save interesting positions, and immediately track their applications without switching between multiple platforms. The Quick Import feature also allows users to paste a job URL and automatically fill in company name, job title, and other details, saving time during the application process.

Can I track interviews with My Job Trackr?

Yes, My Job Trackr includes comprehensive interview tracking features. Users can schedule interviews with specific dates and times, set reminder notifications to ensure they never miss an interview, view all upcoming interviews in an integrated calendar view, add interview notes and preparation materials, and track multiple interview rounds for the same position. The dashboard provides a clear overview of all scheduled interviews.

Does My Job Trackr have a mobile app?

Yes, My Job Trackr has a native iOS mobile app available on the App Store. The iOS app includes all web features including job searching, application tracking, interview management, and offer comparison. Users can access their job search data across both web and mobile platforms as all information syncs automatically.

What is the Quick Import feature?

Quick Import is a My Job Trackr feature that allows users to paste a job posting URL and automatically extract key details including company name, job title, location, and job description. This feature saves time when adding new applications by eliminating manual data entry. Quick Import works with most major job boards and company career pages.

How do I compare job offers with My Job Trackr?

My Job Trackr provides a dedicated offer comparison tool that displays multiple job offers side-by-side. Users can compare salary, benefits, location, company culture, and other factors across different offers. The comparison view helps job seekers make informed decisions by visualising all offer details in one place, making it easier to evaluate trade-offs between different opportunities.

Does My Job Trackr offer a free trial?

Yes, My Job Trackr offers a 7-day free trial for all new users. The trial includes full access to all features including unlimited job tracking, native job searching, interview management, and offer comparison tools. No credit card is required to start the trial. After the trial period, users can choose to subscribe for ¬£2.99/month on web or ¬£3.99/month on iOS.

Who is My Job Trackr designed for?

My Job Trackr is designed for job seekers, recent graduates, and career switchers who need to organise multiple job applications. The app is particularly helpful for active job seekers applying to many positions simultaneously, recent graduates entering the job market for the first time, career switchers managing applications across different industries, and professionals who want to track their job search progress systematically.

Can I access My Job Trackr from multiple devices?

Yes, My Job Trackr syncs your data across all devices. Users can access their job tracking information from the web application on any computer and from the iOS mobile app. All applications, interviews, and offers are automatically synchronised, allowing job seekers to manage their job search from whichever device is most convenient at the time.
