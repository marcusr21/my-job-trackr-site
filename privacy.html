Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Introduction

My Job Trackr ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our web application at app.myjobtrackr.com and our mobile applications (collectively, the "Service").

Our commitment: Your job search data is personal and sensitive. We only collect what's necessary to provide our service, we never sell your data, and you can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Name – to personalise your experience
  • Email address – for account authentication and communications
  • Password – stored securely using industry-standard hashing (never in plain text)

Job Application Data

You provide this information when tracking your job applications:

  • Job titles, company names, and descriptions
  • Locations and office policies (remote, hybrid, on-site)
  • Salary information and expectations
  • Application dates and status updates
  • Interview details (dates, times, types, locations, notes)
  • Offer details (salary, bonus, decision deadlines)
  • Job posting URLs you choose to save

Technical Data

We automatically collect certain technical information:

Data Type Purpose Retention
IP Address Security, fraud prevention, and job search API requirements Session duration
Browser/Device Info Optimising the experience for your device Session duration
Session Data Keeping you logged in securely Until logout or 120 minutes of inactivity
Usage Analytics Improving our service (via Hotjar) As per Hotjar's policy

Payment Information

If you subscribe to a paid plan, payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe. We do not store your credit card numbers, bank details, or other payment credentials on our servers. We only receive confirmation of payment status from Stripe.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the Service – storing and displaying your job applications, interviews, and offers
  • Authenticate your account – ensuring only you can access your data
  • Enable job search features – connecting you to job listings via our integrated search
  • Process payments – managing subscriptions through Stripe
  • Send service communications – account verification, security alerts, and important updates
  • Improve our Service – understanding usage patterns to enhance features
  • Provide customer support – responding to your enquiries

We do not: Sell your data to third parties, share your job application details with employers, or use your data for advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services:

CareerJet (Job Search)

Our job search feature is powered by the CareerJet API. When you search for jobs, the following is shared with CareerJet:

  • Your search terms (keywords, location)
  • Your IP address (required by CareerJet's terms of service)
  • Your browser user agent

This data is used solely to return relevant job listings. CareerJet's privacy policy governs their use of this data: CareerJet Privacy Policy.

Stripe (Payments)

Payment processing is handled by Stripe. When you subscribe to a paid plan, Stripe collects and processes your payment information according to their privacy policy: Stripe Privacy Policy.

Hotjar (Analytics)

We use Hotjar to understand how users interact with our website. Hotjar may collect anonymised usage data. You can opt out via their Do Not Track page.

Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your data seriously:

  • Encryption – All data is transmitted using TLS/SSL encryption
  • Password Security – Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms and never stored in plain text
  • Database Security – Your data is stored in secured databases with access controls
  • Session Management – Sessions expire after periods of inactivity
  • Regular Updates – We keep our systems updated with security patches

While we implement robust security measures, no system is completely secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and UK data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access – Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification – Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Right to Erasure – Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Portability – Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Restrict Processing – Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Object – Object to processing of your data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent – Withdraw consent at any time where we rely on consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address below. We will respond within 30 days.

Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Account Data – Retained while your account is active
  • Job Application Data – Retained while your account is active
  • Deleted Applications – Soft-deleted data is permanently removed within 30 days
  • Session Data – Automatically cleared upon logout or expiry

When you delete your account, all your personal data and job application records are permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days.

Mobile Applications

Our iOS and Android applications (currently in development) access the same data as our web application. The mobile apps may additionally request:

  • Push Notifications – To remind you of upcoming interviews (optional)
  • Calendar Access – To add interviews to your device calendar (optional)

All permissions are optional and can be managed through your device settings. The mobile apps do not collect any additional personal data beyond what is described in this policy.

Cookies

We use essential cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in to your account
  • Remember your session preferences
  • Protect against cross-site request forgery (CSRF)

These cookies are necessary for the Service to function and cannot be disabled. We also use analytics cookies (Hotjar) which you can opt out of as described above.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
